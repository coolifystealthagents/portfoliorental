What Is a DSCR Loan?

A Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) loan qualifies you based on the property's income rather than your personal income. The lender looks at whether the rental income covers the mortgage payment, not your W-2 or tax returns.

DSCR = Monthly Rental Income / Monthly Mortgage Payment (PITIA)

A DSCR of 1.0 means the rent exactly covers the payment. Most lenders want 1.0 or higher, with better terms offered at 1.25+. Running a cash flow analysis before applying helps you confirm your property meets the threshold.

Why DSCR Loans Matter for Small Portfolio Owners

Traditional conventional loans cap out at 10 financed properties. Once you hit that ceiling, DSCR loans become essential for continued growth. They are a key part of rental property financing for investors looking to build a rental portfolio. But many DSCR lenders focus on experienced investors with large portfolios, leaving small portfolio owners underserved.

The five lenders below actively work with investors who have 1-10 properties.

1. Kiavi

Kiavi (formerly LendingHome) is one of the largest DSCR lenders in the market and actively serves small portfolio owners.

Key Details:

  • Minimum DSCR: 1.0 (better rates at 1.25+)
  • Loan amounts: $100,000 - $3,000,000
  • LTV: Up to 80%
  • Minimum credit score: 680
  • Closing timeline: 2-3 weeks
  • Prepayment penalty: 3-year stepdown (3/2/1)

Best for: Investors who want a streamlined digital application process and competitive rates.

2. Visio Lending

Visio is a dedicated rental loan provider that has funded over $2.5 billion in investor loans. They are one of the few lenders that will fund properties in rural areas.

Key Details:

  • Minimum DSCR: 1.0
  • Loan amounts: $75,000 - $2,000,000
  • LTV: Up to 80%
  • Minimum credit score: 680
  • Closing timeline: 3-4 weeks
  • Prepayment penalty: 5-year or 3-year options

Best for: Investors with properties outside major metros or in secondary markets.

3. Lima One Capital

Lima One offers both DSCR rental loans and fix-and-flip financing, making them a good one-stop shop for BRRRR investors.

Key Details:

  • Minimum DSCR: 1.0 (no-ratio options available at lower LTV)
  • Loan amounts: $75,000 - $5,000,000
  • LTV: Up to 80%
  • Minimum credit score: 660
  • Closing timeline: 2-4 weeks
  • Prepayment penalty: Varies by program

Best for: BRRRR investors who want one lender for both the acquisition/rehab and the long-term refinance.

4. Easy Street Capital

Easy Street is a newer lender that has built a reputation for fast closings and working with newer investors.

Key Details:

  • Minimum DSCR: 0.75 (with rate adjustments below 1.0)
  • Loan amounts: $75,000 - $2,000,000
  • LTV: Up to 80%
  • Minimum credit score: 660
  • Closing timeline: 2-3 weeks
  • Prepayment penalty: Flexible options

Best for: Newer investors or properties with slightly lower DSCR ratios that might not qualify elsewhere.

5. CoreVest (Redwood Trust)

CoreVest serves both individual rental loans and portfolio blanket loans, making them useful as your portfolio grows.

Key Details:

  • Minimum DSCR: 1.0
  • Loan amounts: $100,000 - $50,000,000+
  • LTV: Up to 75%
  • Minimum credit score: 680
  • Closing timeline: 3-5 weeks
  • Prepayment penalty: Varies by program

Best for: Investors planning to scale their rental business beyond 10 properties and who want a lender that grows with them.

How to Choose the Right DSCR Lender

Use a rental property ROI calculator to compare the true cost of each lender's terms. Consider these factors when comparing lenders:

Rate vs. Terms

A lower interest rate is not always the best deal. Compare the total cost of the loan including:

  • Origination fees (typically 1-2 points)
  • Prepayment penalty structure
  • Required reserves
  • Closing costs

Prepayment Penalties

Most DSCR loans include prepayment penalties. Common structures:

  • 5-year stepdown: 5/4/3/2/1% of balance
  • 3-year stepdown: 3/2/1% of balance
  • Yield maintenance: More complex calculation, usually more expensive

If you plan to refinance within 3-5 years, a shorter prepayment penalty is worth a slightly higher rate.

Seasoning Requirements

If you are doing a BRRRR refinance, check the lender's seasoning requirements. Some require 6+ months of ownership before they will lend based on the appraised value rather than the purchase price.

Tips for Getting Approved

  1. Have your rent documentation ready — Lease agreements or market rent estimates from a licensed appraiser
  2. Maintain a credit score above 700 — The best rates start at 720+
  3. Keep 6-12 months of reserves — Most lenders require reserves for each financed property
  4. Get a property inspection done — Lenders may require this and it speeds up the process
  5. Work with a mortgage broker — Brokers can shop multiple DSCR lenders at once and find the best fit

The Bottom Line

DSCR loans are the primary tool for scaling a rental portfolio beyond conventional loan limits, whether you focus on multi-family or single-family properties. Start building relationships with these lenders now, even if you are not ready to borrow yet. Understanding their requirements will help you structure your next acquisition for smooth financing. Use a mortgage calculator to model different loan scenarios before you apply.
