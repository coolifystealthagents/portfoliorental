25:T3441,<p>Effective guest communication is one of the highest-leverage activities a short-term rental host can invest in. A single well-timed, warm message can be the difference between a three-star review and a glowing five-star one. Yet most hosts underestimate how much goes into a polished communication flow — or burn out trying to write every message from scratch.</p>
<p>This guide gives you a complete framework for <strong>guest communication in short-term rentals</strong>: what to say, when to say it, and how to automate the process so your guests feel taken care of without you glued to your phone 24/7.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="why-guest-communication-makes-or-breaks-your-str-business">Why Guest Communication Makes or Breaks Your STR Business</h2>
<p>Guests judge their stay before they even walk through the door. From the first inquiry response to the post-checkout thank-you, every message shapes their perception of your professionalism. Research from the vacation rental industry consistently shows that response time and communication quality are among the top factors guests cite in reviews.</p>
<p>Here is what strong guest communication accomplishes:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Reduces pre-arrival anxiety</strong> — guests who know exactly what to expect check in with confidence</li>
<li><strong>Prevents avoidable issues</strong> — proactive messages catch problems before they become complaints</li>
<li><strong>Boosts review scores</strong> — guests who feel cared for leave better reviews</li>
<li><strong>Saves you time</strong> — templates and automation mean fewer back-and-forth questions</li>
<li><strong>Builds repeat bookings</strong> — warm, personal communication creates loyalty</li>
</ul>
<p>For a deeper look at the operational side of hosting, see our <a href="/blog/short-term-rental-management-guide">short-term rental management guide</a>.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-7-core-messages-every-str-host-needs">The 7 Core Messages Every STR Host Needs</h2>
<h3 id="1-inquiry-response-template">1. Inquiry Response Template</h3>
<p>Whether you use instant book or manual approval, every inquiry deserves a fast, friendly reply.</p>
<p><strong>When to send:</strong> Within 1 hour of inquiry (or immediately via automation)</p>
<p><strong>Template:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Hi [Guest Name],</p>
<p>Thanks so much for reaching out about [Property Name]! We'd love to host you for your trip to [City/Area].</p>
<p>Our place is a great fit for [guests traveling for X, families, couples, etc.]. Just to confirm a few details — you'll be staying [dates] with [number] guests. Everything looks good on our end!</p>
<p>Feel free to ask any questions before booking. We respond quickly and want you to feel completely comfortable.</p>
<p>Looking forward to hosting you,
[Your Name]</p>
</blockquote>
<hr>
<h3 id="2-booking-confirmation-template">2. Booking Confirmation Template</h3>
<p><strong>When to send:</strong> Immediately after booking is confirmed</p>
<p><strong>Template:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Hi [Guest Name],</p>
<p>Your booking is confirmed — we're so excited to host you at [Property Name]!</p>
<p>Here's a quick summary:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Check-in:</strong> [Date] after [Time]</li>
<li><strong>Check-out:</strong> [Date] by [Time]</li>
<li><strong>Address:</strong> [Full address — or "I'll send this closer to your arrival date"]</li>
</ul>
<p>About a week before your stay, I'll send over everything you need: check-in instructions, door codes, house rules, and local tips. In the meantime, don't hesitate to reach out with any questions.</p>
<p>Safe travels,
[Your Name]</p>
</blockquote>
<hr>
<h3 id="3-pre-arrival-message-5-7-days-before-check-in">3. Pre-Arrival Message (5–7 Days Before Check-In)</h3>
<p><strong>When to send:</strong> 5 to 7 days before check-in</p>
<p><strong>Template:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Hi [Guest Name],</p>
<p>Your stay at [Property Name] is just around the corner! Here's everything you need to have a smooth arrival:</p>
<p><strong>Check-In Instructions:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>The property is located at [Full Address]</li>
<li>Access code / lockbox: [Code]</li>
<li>Parking: [Details]</li>
<li>Wi-Fi: Network: [Name] / Password: [Password]</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>A few quick notes:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Check-in is after [Time]. If you're arriving earlier or later, please let me know.</li>
<li>You'll find a welcome book in the entryway with local restaurant picks, house tips, and emergency contacts.</li>
</ul>
<p>I want this to be a fantastic stay for you. Reach me anytime at [Phone/App].</p>
<p>See you soon!
[Your Name]</p>
</blockquote>
<hr>
<h3 id="4-check-in-day-message">4. Check-In Day Message</h3>
<p><strong>When to send:</strong> Morning of check-in day</p>
<p><strong>Template:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Good morning [Guest Name]! Today's the day — welcome to [City]!</p>
<p>Just a quick reminder that you can check in after [Time]. Your door code is [Code].</p>
<p>If you run into any issues at all, text me directly at [Number] and I'll get back to you right away.</p>
<p>Have a wonderful stay!
[Your Name]</p>
</blockquote>
<hr>
<h3 id="5-mid-stay-check-in-message">5. Mid-Stay Check-In Message</h3>
<p><strong>When to send:</strong> Day 2 of the stay (or after the first night for shorter stays)</p>
<p><strong>Template:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Hi [Guest Name],</p>
<p>Hope you're settling in and enjoying [City]! Just checking in to make sure everything is going well at the property.</p>
<p>Is there anything you need or anything I can help with? I want your stay to be as comfortable as possible.</p>
<p>Enjoy your time!
[Your Name]</p>
</blockquote>
<p>This mid-stay touchpoint is one of the most overlooked tools for catching small issues before they become negative reviews. If something is wrong, it gives guests a chance to tell you — rather than telling Airbnb.</p>
<hr>
<h3 id="6-pre-checkout-reminder">6. Pre-Checkout Reminder</h3>
<p><strong>When to send:</strong> Evening before checkout (or morning of, for late-night stays)</p>
<p><strong>Template:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Hi [Guest Name],</p>
<p>Hope you've had a wonderful stay! Just a quick reminder that checkout is tomorrow by [Time].</p>
<p><strong>Checkout steps:</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Strip the beds and leave used towels in the bathroom</li>
<li>Load and start the dishwasher</li>
<li>Take out any trash</li>
<li>Lock the door and leave the key [in lockbox / on counter]</li>
</ol>
<p>Full checkout instructions are in the welcome book and the listing.</p>
<p>Safe travels wherever you're headed next. It was a pleasure hosting you!
[Your Name]</p>
</blockquote>
<p>For a complete template, see our <a href="/blog/checkout-instructions-template">checkout instructions template</a>.</p>
<hr>
<h3 id="7-post-checkout-review-request">7. Post-Checkout Review Request</h3>
<p><strong>When to send:</strong> 1–2 hours after checkout time</p>
<p><strong>Template:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Hi [Guest Name],</p>
<p>Thanks so much for staying at [Property Name]! I hope you had a great trip.</p>
<p>If you have a moment, I'd really appreciate it if you left a review — it helps other travelers know what to expect and supports our small hosting business. I'll be leaving you a glowing review as well!</p>
<p>Hope to see you again next time you're in [City].</p>
<p>Warmly,
[Your Name]</p>
</blockquote>
<hr>
<h2 id="automating-your-guest-communication">Automating Your Guest Communication</h2>
<p>Writing great templates is only half the battle. The real leverage comes from automating them so they go out on schedule without any manual effort.</p>
<h3 id="top-tools-for-str-message-automation">Top Tools for STR Message Automation</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Hospitable (formerly Smartbnb)</strong> — rule-based messaging automation with merge fields</li>
<li><strong>Hostaway</strong> — multi-channel property management with built-in automation</li>
<li><strong>Guesty</strong> — enterprise-grade automation for portfolio hosts</li>
<li><strong>Airbnb's Scheduled Messages</strong> — native feature for simple automation within the Airbnb platform</li>
</ul>
<p>To explore the full landscape of time-saving tools, read our guide to <a href="/blog/airbnb-automation-tools">Airbnb automation tools</a>.</p>
<h3 id="setting-up-automation-best-practices">Setting Up Automation: Best Practices</h3>
<ol>
<li><strong>Use merge fields</strong> — always personalize with guest name, property name, and dates</li>
<li><strong>Build in a delay</strong> — don't send the checkout message the second they leave; give it 30–60 minutes</li>
<li><strong>Flag exceptions</strong> — set alerts for short stays, large groups, or special requests that need manual attention</li>
<li><strong>Review monthly</strong> — templates get stale; update seasonal tips and local recommendations regularly</li>
</ol>
<hr>

<h2 id="communication-etiquette-dos-and-don-ts">Communication Etiquette: Dos and Don'ts</h2>
<p><strong>Do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Respond within 1 hour during business hours. The <a href="https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/manage-your-business/manage-your-customers">SBA recommends prompt communication</a> with customers.</li>
<li>Match the guest's energy (warm guests get warmer replies)</li>
<li>Be proactive — send updates before guests ask</li>
<li>Use the guest's name in every message</li>
<li>Keep messages concise and scannable</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Don't:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Send walls of text that overwhelm guests</li>
<li>Go dark during a guest's stay</li>
<li>Promise things you can't deliver (early check-in, amenities not listed)</li>
<li>Use overly formal or robotic language</li>
<li>Wait for a complaint — check in proactively</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h2 id="managing-reviews-through-communication">Managing Reviews Through Communication</h2>
<p>Your communication style directly affects your review scores. Guests who feel heard and valued almost always leave better reviews — even when minor issues occur. The mid-stay check-in message is your secret weapon here: it catches problems while you can still fix them.</p>
<p>For strategies to actively build your review score, see our guide to <a href="/blog/five-star-review-strategy-airbnb">five-star review strategies on Airbnb</a>. And for handling the reviews that come in, our <a href="/blog/airbnb-review-response-template">Airbnb review response templates</a> show you exactly what to say.</p>
<p>According to Airbnb's host resources, response rate and response time are two of the most significant factors in achieving and maintaining Superhost status. <a href="https://www.forbes.com/advisor/investing/best-vacation-rental-companies/">Forbes covers vacation rental</a> hosting best practices — making fast, consistent communication a direct business advantage.</p>
