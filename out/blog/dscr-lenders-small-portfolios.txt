20:T1bad,<h2 id="what-is-a-dscr-loan">What Is a DSCR Loan?</h2>
<p>A <a href="https://www.investopedia.com/terms/d/dscr.asp" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Debt Service Coverage Ratio</a> (DSCR) loan qualifies you based on the property's income rather than your personal income. The lender looks at whether the rental income covers the mortgage payment, not your W-2 or tax returns.</p>
<p><strong>DSCR = Monthly Rental Income / Monthly Mortgage Payment (PITIA)</strong></p>
<p>A DSCR of 1.0 means the rent exactly covers the payment. Most lenders want 1.0 or higher, with better terms offered at 1.25+. Running a <a href="/blog/rental-property-cash-flow-analysis">cash flow analysis</a> before applying helps you confirm your property meets the threshold.</p>
<h2 id="why-dscr-loans-matter-for-small-portfolio-owners">Why DSCR Loans Matter for Small Portfolio Owners</h2>
<p>Traditional conventional loans cap out at 10 financed properties. Once you hit that ceiling, DSCR loans become essential for continued growth. They are a key part of <a href="/blog/rental-property-financing-options">rental property financing</a> for investors looking to <a href="/blog/build-rental-property-portfolio">build a rental portfolio</a>. But many DSCR lenders focus on experienced investors with large portfolios, leaving small portfolio owners underserved.</p>
<p>The five lenders below actively work with investors who have 1-10 properties.</p>
<h2 id="1-kiavi">1. Kiavi</h2>
<p>Kiavi (formerly LendingHome) is one of the largest DSCR lenders in the market and actively serves small portfolio owners.</p>
<p><strong>Key Details:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum DSCR: 1.0 (better rates at 1.25+)</li>
<li>Loan amounts: $100,000 - $3,000,000</li>
<li>LTV: Up to 80%</li>
<li>Minimum credit score: 680</li>
<li>Closing timeline: 2-3 weeks</li>
<li>Prepayment penalty: 3-year stepdown (3/2/1)</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Best for:</strong> Investors who want a streamlined digital application process and competitive rates.</p>
<h2 id="2-visio-lending">2. Visio Lending</h2>
<p>Visio is a dedicated rental loan provider that has funded over $2.5 billion in investor loans. They are one of the few lenders that will fund properties in rural areas.</p>
<p><strong>Key Details:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum DSCR: 1.0</li>
<li>Loan amounts: $75,000 - $2,000,000</li>
<li>LTV: Up to 80%</li>
<li>Minimum credit score: 680</li>
<li>Closing timeline: 3-4 weeks</li>
<li>Prepayment penalty: 5-year or 3-year options</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Best for:</strong> Investors with properties outside major metros or in secondary markets.</p>
<h2 id="3-lima-one-capital">3. Lima One Capital</h2>
<p>Lima One offers both DSCR rental loans and fix-and-flip financing, making them a good one-stop shop for BRRRR investors.</p>
<p><strong>Key Details:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum DSCR: 1.0 (no-ratio options available at lower LTV)</li>
<li>Loan amounts: $75,000 - $5,000,000</li>
<li>LTV: Up to 80%</li>
<li>Minimum credit score: 660</li>
<li>Closing timeline: 2-4 weeks</li>
<li>Prepayment penalty: Varies by program</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Best for:</strong> BRRRR investors who want one lender for both the acquisition/rehab and the long-term refinance.</p>
<h2 id="4-easy-street-capital">4. Easy Street Capital</h2>
<p>Easy Street is a newer lender that has built a reputation for fast closings and working with newer investors.</p>
<p><strong>Key Details:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum DSCR: 0.75 (with rate adjustments below 1.0)</li>
<li>Loan amounts: $75,000 - $2,000,000</li>
<li>LTV: Up to 80%</li>
<li>Minimum credit score: 660</li>
<li>Closing timeline: 2-3 weeks</li>
<li>Prepayment penalty: Flexible options</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Best for:</strong> Newer investors or properties with slightly lower DSCR ratios that might not qualify elsewhere.</p>
<h2 id="5-corevest-redwood-trust">5. CoreVest (Redwood Trust)</h2>
<p>CoreVest serves both individual rental loans and portfolio blanket loans, making them useful as your portfolio grows.</p>
<p><strong>Key Details:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum DSCR: 1.0</li>
<li>Loan amounts: $100,000 - $50,000,000+</li>
<li>LTV: Up to 75%</li>
<li>Minimum credit score: 680</li>
<li>Closing timeline: 3-5 weeks</li>
<li>Prepayment penalty: Varies by program</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Best for:</strong> Investors planning to <a href="/blog/scaling-rental-property-business">scale their rental business</a> beyond 10 properties and who want a lender that grows with them.</p>
<h2 id="how-to-choose-the-right-dscr-lender">How to Choose the Right DSCR Lender</h2>
<p>Use a <a href="/blog/rental-property-roi-calculator">rental property ROI calculator</a> to compare the true cost of each lender's terms. Consider these factors when comparing lenders:</p>
<h3 id="rate-vs-terms">Rate vs. Terms</h3>
<p>A lower interest rate is not always the best deal. Compare the total cost of the loan including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Origination fees (typically 1-2 points)</li>
<li>Prepayment penalty structure</li>
<li>Required reserves</li>
<li>Closing costs</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="prepayment-penalties">Prepayment Penalties</h3>
<p>Most DSCR loans include prepayment penalties. Common structures:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>5-year stepdown:</strong> 5/4/3/2/1% of balance</li>
<li><strong>3-year stepdown:</strong> 3/2/1% of balance</li>
<li><strong>Yield maintenance:</strong> More complex calculation, usually more expensive</li>
</ul>
<p>If you plan to refinance within 3-5 years, a shorter prepayment penalty is worth a slightly higher rate.</p>
<h3 id="seasoning-requirements">Seasoning Requirements</h3>
<p>If you are doing a BRRRR refinance, check the lender's seasoning requirements. Some require 6+ months of ownership before they will lend based on the appraised value rather than the purchase price.</p>
<h2 id="tips-for-getting-approved">Tips for Getting Approved</h2>
<ol>
<li><strong>Have your rent documentation ready</strong> — Lease agreements or market rent estimates from a licensed appraiser</li>
<li><strong>Maintain a credit score above 700</strong> — The best rates start at 720+</li>
<li><strong>Keep 6-12 months of reserves</strong> — Most lenders require reserves for each financed property</li>
<li><strong>Get a property inspection done</strong> — Lenders may require this and it speeds up the process</li>
<li><strong>Work with a mortgage broker</strong> — Brokers can shop multiple DSCR lenders at once and find the best fit</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="the-bottom-line">The Bottom Line</h2>
<p>DSCR loans are the primary tool for scaling a rental portfolio beyond conventional loan limits, whether you focus on <a href="/blog/multi-family-vs-single-family-investing">multi-family or single-family properties</a>. Start building relationships with these lenders now, even if you are not ready to borrow yet. Understanding their requirements will help you structure your next acquisition for smooth financing. Use a <a href="https://www.bankrate.com/mortgages/mortgage-calculator/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">mortgage calculator</a> to model different loan scenarios before you apply.</p>
