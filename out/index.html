Frequently Asked Questions

How much does property management cost?

Our full-service property management starts at 8% of monthly rental income. Pricing varies based on portfolio size, property type, and services needed. Most clients find we pay for ourselves through reduced vacancy and optimized rental rates.

What types of properties do you manage?

We manage single-family homes, multi-family apartments, townhouses, condos, and small commercial properties. Whether you have one rental or a portfolio of 100+, our services scale to fit your needs.

How do you screen tenants?

Every applicant goes through credit checks, criminal background screening, employment and income verification, previous landlord references, and rental history review. Our process is Fair Housing compliant and designed to find reliable, long-term tenants.

Can I try your services before committing long-term?

Yes! We offer month-to-month management agreements with no long-term contracts required. You can evaluate our performance before making a longer commitment.

What markets do you serve?

We provide property management services across multiple markets. Contact us to check availability in your area and learn about our local market expertise.

How do I access my financial reports?

Every owner gets access to our online owner portal where you can view real-time financial data, monthly statements, maintenance updates, and year-end tax documents — available 24/7.
