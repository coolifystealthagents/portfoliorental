Frequently Asked Questions

How does hourly pricing work?
You're billed only for the hours your property manager works on your portfolio. We track time transparently and provide detailed breakdowns in your monthly statement.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No minimum commitment required. You can start month-to-month and upgrade to a commitment plan anytime for additional savings.
Can I switch tiers?
Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What's included in the hourly rate?
Everything listed in your tier's features. There are no hidden fees — the hourly rate covers all standard management activities.
Do you handle maintenance emergencies?
Yes. All tiers include 24/7 emergency hotline access. Emergency response time varies by tier, with Gold and Diamond getting priority.
How do commitment discounts work?
Choose a 3, 6, or 12-month commitment when you sign up. Your hourly rate is discounted for the duration. No penalty if circumstances change — we'll pro-rate any difference.
